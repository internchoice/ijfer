Refund Policy

Publication fees are generally non-refundable once the paper has been accepted. In case of special circumstances, please contact our editorial team. Refund requests for rejected manuscripts (before acceptance) may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Invoicing

An invoice will be provided upon payment. Authors can request a formal invoice for institutional reimbursement or record-keeping purposes.
