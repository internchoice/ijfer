Yes. There is a publication fee for accepted papers in the journal. The publication processing fee for a research paper is INR 599 (Without DOI) or INR 799 (With DOI) for Indian authors and $23 (Without DOI) or $27 (With DOI) for foreign authors. The author/corresponding author will get published papers and soft copies of certificates. However, the author may order hard copies of certificates. The charge for hard copies of certificates and papers depends on the number of pages in papers and certificates. The publication fee is charged only for accepted papers.
