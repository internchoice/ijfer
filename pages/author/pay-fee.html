Paper Publication or Processing Charges

INDIAN JOURNAL FOR ENGINEERING AND RESEARCH & Science is an Open Access journal. IJFER does not gain any income through selling online versions of the published articles. IJFER enables its readers to access the published articles free of cost.

IJFER allows its authors and readers to distribute knowledge more widely all over the world at a lower cost. However, to cover the peer review work, Administrative support, Journal development, Formatting of articles, Marketing of journal, Customer service, Operation and Maintenance of online journal system and website, DOI services, the journal depends on Article Processing Fees.
