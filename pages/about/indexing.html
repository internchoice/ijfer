Benefits of Indexing

Enhanced Visibility

Research reaches wider academic and professional audiences through major indexing databases.

Improved Citations

Indexed articles receive higher citation rates due to better discoverability and accessibility.

Academic Recognition

Indexing in reputable databases enhances the academic credibility and impact of published work.

Indexing Policy

IJFER actively pursues indexing in major academic databases to maximize the visibility and impact of published research. We:

  • Submit metadata to indexing services regularly
  • Maintain high-quality standards for all published content
  • Ensure proper DOI assignment for all articles
  • Comply with indexing database requirements and standards
  • Work continuously to expand our indexing coverage

For inquiries about indexing or to suggest new databases for inclusion, please contact our editorial team at editor@IJFER.com.
