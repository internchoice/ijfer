Publication Ethics

IJFER adheres to strict publication ethics. Our policies cover plagiarism prevention, duplicate submission restrictions, authorship integrity, and handling of research misconduct. We follow COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines to ensure all research is evaluated fairly and ethically.

Authors are expected to conduct responsible research and adhere to ethical standards in their submissions. The journal reserves the right to reject or retract manuscripts that violate these principles.
