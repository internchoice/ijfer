Editor Vision

IJFER invites review papers, final papers, technical reports, scientific, and research articles from authors to submit in all areas of engineering, technology, science, environmental science, management, business, medical science, and all other fields of study or research. Submitted papers and articles are subjected to a peer review process. Papers/articles may be experimental, numerical analysis, theoretical, or interpretative. Our journal aims to create a new forum for the exchange of ideas and information on all aspects of research, engineering, technology, and science. We provide superior quality editorial, review, and support, having the most talented and professional team on ground and offshore.

IJFER is a key factor in raising the standards of discussion, evaluation and analyses relating to topics of engineering, science, management, technology and other research area. It also provides a platform for professional, engineers, students, researchers, academicians and practitioners who share their knowledge in the field of engineering, science, management, technology, industrial organization and other relevant disciplines from all over the world. IJFER publishes original papers, review papers, research articles, thesis and case studies with significant analysis results and technical notes in related areas and their applications.
