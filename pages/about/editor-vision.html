Editor Vision

The editorial team at IJFER is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity and research excellence. Our long-term goal is to create a trusted platform that advances scientific knowledge through rigorous peer review and ethical publishing practices.

We believe in fostering innovation in research publishing and supporting researchers at all stages of their careers. The Editor-in-Chief and our team emphasize the importance of transparent, unbiased peer review and the journal's role in disseminating quality research globally.
