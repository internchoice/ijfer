Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo a structured peer review workflow. Manuscripts are assessed by expert reviewers in the relevant field. The process includes initial screening, blind peer review, editorial decision, and final publication. We aim for transparency and thorough evaluation.

Typical review timelines: Initial screening within 24 hours, peer review feedback within 2-4 weeks, and publication within 4 hours after acceptance upon fee payment and copyright form submission.
