IJFER is a peer-reviewed, Open Access, low cost journal with fast processing that publishes original research articles as well as review articles in several areas of engineering, science and technology for the enhancement of scientific research work. IJFER enables its readers to access the published articles free of cost which helps in spreading knowledge, thereby guaranteeing scholars, scientists, academicians, engineers and students of engineering and technology for reading, reprinting, and distributing to facilitate academic and research activities.
