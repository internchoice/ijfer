Author Guidelines

1. Publication Process

Our publication workflow is transparent and efficient. The process includes:

  • Initial Screening: Manuscript received and acknowledged within 6 hours
  • Plagiarism Check: All submissions undergo strict plagiarism screening
  • Peer Review: Expert reviewers evaluate your manuscript (typically 24 hours for initial response)
  • Editorial Decision: Accept, Revise, or Reject
  • Final Publication: Within 4 hours after fee payment and copyright form receipt

2. Submit Paper Online

Upload your manuscript directly through our secure online portal. Supported formats: PDF, DOCX.

Online Submission Form

3. Pay Publication Fee

After acceptance, pay the article processing charge (APC) through our secure gateway. Accepted methods: Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, BHIM).

View Fee Structure

4. Track Paper

Monitor your manuscript status using the Paper ID provided upon submission.

Status options: Under Review | Revision Requested | Accepted | Published

5. Copyright Form

Download the copyright transfer agreement, complete it, and submit along with payment. This transfers publication rights to the journal while retaining ethical and legal compliance.
