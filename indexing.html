Indexing Policy

IJFER actively pursues indexing in major academic databases to maximize the visibility and impact of published research. Our comprehensive indexing strategy includes:

  • Regular Submissions: We submit metadata to indexing services on a monthly basis to ensure timely inclusion of new publications
  • Quality Standards: Maintain rigorous quality standards for all published content to meet indexing database requirements
  • DOI Management: Ensure proper DOI assignment and registration for all articles through Crossref
  • Compliance: Adhere to indexing database requirements, standards, and best practices for metadata quality
  • Continuous Expansion: Actively work to expand our indexing coverage in premium databases and new platforms
  • Performance Monitoring: Track indexing performance and citation metrics to optimize visibility strategies
  • Author Benefits: Provide authors with indexing information and citation tracking for their published work
For Authors

All published articles automatically receive indexing benefits. Authors can track their article's indexing status and citation metrics through our author portal. For specific inquiries about indexing or to suggest new databases for inclusion, please contact our editorial team.
